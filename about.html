About This World

A fan-made digital space inspired by Naomi Scott’s music, artistry, and creative energy.

Why this site exists

This site began as a fun fan project while waiting for Naomi Scott’s new album, but it quickly became something more special to me. What started as a playful countdown turned into a growing digital world where I can create Naomi-inspired coding projects, interactive pages, and little games.

I wanted this space to feel soft, fun, and imaginative — a place where music inspiration can turn into something visual, playful, and interactive.

What lives here

mini games fan-inspired pages creative coding digital experiments future projects

This world is where I’ll keep all of my Naomi Scott-inspired coding and digital projects. That includes games, playful interfaces, themed web pages, and whatever new ideas I dream up along the way.

A growing archive

Even after the album comes out, this site will stay live and continue growing. I want it to become a little archive of Naomi-inspired creative projects — something I can keep adding to over time as I make more things.

Everything here is fan-made and created with love, curiosity, and a little bit of whimsy ♡
