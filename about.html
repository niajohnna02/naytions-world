Why this site exists

This site began as a fun fan project while waiting for Naomi Scott's new album, but it quickly became something more special to me. What started as a playful countdown turned into a growing digital world where I can create Naomi-inspired coding projects, interactive pages, and little games.

I wanted this space to feel soft, fun, and imaginative — a place where music inspiration can turn into something visual, playful, and interactive.
